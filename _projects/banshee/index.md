## Overview

Banshee is a 1lb **drum spinner** combat robot built as part of the **WPI Combat Robotics Club**. The chassis and weapon system are fully 3D-printed, requiring careful attention to print orientation and geometry to survive high-impact collisions.

---

## Design & Manufacturing

The core challenge was designing a fully printed robot that could survive repeated weapon impacts. Key considerations included:

- **Print orientation** — surfaces subject to high stress were oriented to maximize layer adhesion in the load direction
- **Geometry optimization** — rounded transitions and thick wall sections to distribute impact forces
- **Drum weapon** — designed for high angular momentum within the 1lb weight limit
